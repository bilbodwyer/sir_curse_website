THE STORY SO FAR

Once upon a time there was a intuitive boy called Phil Donaldson. He was on the way to see his Wenna Johnson, when he decided to take a short cut through Hyde Park.

It wasn't long before Phil got lost. He looked around, but all he could see were trees. Nervously, he felt into his bag for his favourite toy, Terrance the Moose, but Terrance the Moose was nowhere to be found! Phil began to panic. He felt sure he had packed Terrance the Moose. To make matters worse, he was starting to feel hungry.

Unexpectedly, he saw a slippery monkey dressed in a yellow waistcoat disappearing into the trees.

"How odd!" thought Phil.

For the want of anything better to do, he decided to follow the peculiarly dressed monkey. Perhaps it could tell him the way out of the forest.
